Today, you're feeling... anxious

What does this emotion mean?

According to Dictionary.com, being anxious is when one is "full of mental distress or uneasiness because of fear of danger or misfortune; greatly worried; apprehensive."

So, how can you deal with feeling anxious?

  • Meditate and use calming breathing exercises
  • Acknowledge what is making you anxious
  • Take a walk to distract yourself
  • Talk to a mental health professional
