Today, you're feeling... sad

What does this emotion mean?

According to dictionary.com, being sad is when one is "affected by unhappiness or grief; sorrowful or mournful; expressive of or characterized by sorrow; or causing sorrow."

So, how can you deal with feeling sad?

  • Write down your feelings
  • Talk to someone
  • Do something you enjoy
