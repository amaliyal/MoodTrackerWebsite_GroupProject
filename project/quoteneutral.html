Today you're feeling...neutral

What does this emotion mean?

Feeling neutral is when you feel indifferent, impartial and OK. It can be desribed as a settled, non-dramatic emotional space. There is nothing particularly exciting about it, but also nothing bad about it. It can also be a space of contentment and peace as long as we do not think there is something wrong with it.

So, how can you maintain or improve neutral emotions?

Do an activity that you enjoy, such as socialising with friends.

Spend more time outdoors and in nature.

Make sure you are getting enough sleep, this will help you to wake up feeling refreshed and energised!
